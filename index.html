The weekly loop

  • The question is "have you taken this week's injection, at what dose, and how are you tolerating it", not "what did you eat".
  • Log the jab and dose, add your weight, rate your appetite, and flag any side effects, in under a minute.
  • A step-up is offered when it is due, but a doctor confirms it. The app never moves your dose on its own.
  • Progress shows the weight curve and the honest headline: percent of your starting weight lost.
  • The tolerance check quietly watches for the few red-flag symptoms and routes them straight to a person.
